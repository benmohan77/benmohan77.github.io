Chloe the Golden Retriever

We adopted Chloe into our home in August of 2014, after losing our previous wonderful Golden, Lindy. Chloe was one of the largest dogs in her litter but has only grown to be 50 pounds or so. While previous dogs we have had we trained to hunt, we haven't trained Chloe, and judging by her reaction to fireworks, she probably wouldn't do too well.
